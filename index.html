The Tri-State Labyrinth

The Northeast Corridor is the most hostile environment for mobile fueling in North America. [cite_start]This is the single biggest barrier to entry and, conversely, Carmyn's strongest defensive moat. [cite: 101, 103]

NYC

The FDNY Firewall

Every driver needs a Certificate of Fitness (W-14). [cite_start]Fueling is prohibited on public streets and strictly regulated on private lots with 50ft setback requirements. [cite: 106, 111]

NJ

The "Attendant" Mandate

Self-service is illegal (N.J.S.A. 34:3A-4). [cite_start]Carmyn turns this into an advantage: by bringing the "attendant" to the depot, they ensure compliance for fleets that cannot legally fill themselves. [cite: 115, 119]

MA

Marine & State Fire Code

Marine fueling requires approval from local harbormasters and strict spill response plans. [cite_start]Specialized insurance policies with $5M+ coverage are mandatory. [cite: 125, 126]
